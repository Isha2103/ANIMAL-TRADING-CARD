Pomeranian

The Pomeranian (often known as a Pom) is a breed of dog of the Spitz type that is named for the Pomerania region in north-west Poland and north-east Germany in Central Europe.

  • Alias: Zwergspitz
  • Height: 7 to 12 inches
  • Weight: 3 to 7 pounds
  • Lifespan: 12 to 16 years

Pomeranians are alert and aware of changes in their environment.The top coat forms a ruff of fur on the neck, which Poms are well known for, and they also have a fringe of feathery hair on the hindquarters.

Penguin

Penguin jump into the air before diving to swim faster.The move releases air bubbles from their feathers, cutting down on drag and doubling or tripling their speed underwater, according to Smithsonian.

  • Scientific Name: Spheniscidae
  • Average Length: 5 foot
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years
  • Habitat: Penguins are found on every continent in the Southern Hemisphere

Penguins are a group of aquatic flightless birds. Highly adapted for life in the water, penguins have countershaded dark and white plumage and flippers for swimming. Most penguins feed on krill, fish, squid and other forms of sea life which they catch while swimming underwater. They spend roughly half of their lives on land and the other half in the sea.s
